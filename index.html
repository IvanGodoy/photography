About

Hi, I'm Ivan!

I always loved the idea of looking for what's hidden in plain sight. I studied Physics to be able to see the beauty and complexity of the natural world; photography helped me sharing the perspective I developed. In portraits I try to reveal character with light and timing, and at events I catch the fleeting moments of human connection before they disappear.

Gear matters less than light and timing, but if you care: Sony full‑frame bodies with fast primes, color‑calibrated workflow, and backups from card to cloud.
